Handmade Spice Wreaths

We always produce our spice products ourselves. This ensures individuality and quality in one. Customer satisfaction with our handcrafted items is especially important to us.

Production

How a spice product is created:

Selection

First, the spices to be used are selected.

Wiring

All spices are wrapped with wire. This step makes it easier to tie all the spices together.

Binding

All spices are tied together into a bouquet and beautifully arranged.

Finishing

Finally, the finishing touches are done. A decorative cuff is added — and voilà.

Photography

The products are photographed afterward, as each one is individually crafted.

Marketing

Next, we proceed with the marketing campaign.

Sales

Finally, the products are sold at our sales stand.

Support

Additionally, we offer individual support. Do you have special requests? No problem — we do our best to fulfill them.
